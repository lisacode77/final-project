Water adventures for all ages!

Llyn Y Boda Lake is a brand new water-based adventure hub, offering a number of unique, dynamic and adrenaline-charged outdoor activities including our brand new Aqua Park, Sailing, Kayaking, Stand Up Paddleboarding and Open Water Swimming. Located in rural Wales, 12 miles east of Chester, Llyn Y Boda is the perfect day out for friends, families and individuals. Whether you want to give Kayaking a try, take on the challenge of our floating Aqua Park obstacle course or explore our stunning lake on a SUP board, Llyn Y Boda has something for all ages and abilities.
